Version: 0.5.0
Date: 2026-04-26
  Features:
    - Added circuit network connections to Delivery Cannons
    - Delivery Cannons can now use Factorio 2.0's native assembler recipe control via circuit signals
    - Added a Delivery Cannon Selector Combinator that converts item signals into matching delivery cannon recipe signals
    - The Delivery Cannon Selector Combinator unlocks with `se-delivery-cannon`
    - The selector combinator's output is intended for recipe control; its network output is correct even though the entity hover tooltip may still only show the input side
